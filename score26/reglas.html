Rules and score

Group Stage and Qualifiers (up to 4th)

  • Success of Exact result: 5 pints
  • Success of Winner or tie (without exact result): 2 points
  • Elimination Phase: If the match ends tied, points are only added if the winner of the penalty shootout is correct (without extra score).

Special Points (Finals)

You are rewarded if you get the country right, it doesn't matter if the score was accurate

  • Success of Champion: 13 points
  • Success of 2nd place: 10 Points
  • Success of 3rd place: 6 points
  • Success of Best scorer: 8 points
